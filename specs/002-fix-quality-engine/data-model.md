# Phase 1: Domain & Data Model

**Feature**: True LLM Orchestration and Quality Engine
**Branch**: `002-fix-quality-engine`

## Key Entities & Data Structures

### 1. Chat Message (`ChatMessage`)
Represents an incoming user prompt and its resulting classification and response payload within the conversational interface.

#### Fields
- `message_id` (UUID): Unique identifier for the chat interaction.
- `project_id` (str): Identifier of the active story project.
- `raw_prompt` (str): The exact text entered by the user.
- `intent` (IntentType): Classified intent (e.g., `WRITE_CHAPTER`, `GENERAL_CHAT`, `ANSWER_QUESTION`).
- `confidence` (float): AI/Regex confidence score for the classified intent (0.0 to 1.0).
- `extracted_info` (Dict): Structured entities or parameters extracted from the prompt (e.g., target word count).
- `response_text` (str): The final text generated by the assistant.
- `timestamp` (datetime): Timestamp of the interaction.

#### Validation Rules & Constraints
- `raw_prompt` must be non-empty and stripped of leading/trailing whitespace.
- `confidence` must be >= 0.0 and <= 1.0.

---

### 2. Quality Evaluation Rubric (`QualityRubric`)
Represents the 8-criteria scoring breakdown used by the Quality Engine to evaluate manuscript prose.

#### Fields
- `evaluation_id` (UUID): Unique identifier for the evaluation pass.
- `chapter_id` (str): Identifier of the associated chapter draft.
- `narrative_drive` (float): Score for active verb usage and forward plot momentum (0.0 to 10.0).
- `character_voice` (float): Score for distinct dialogue tags and speech patterns (0.0 to 10.0).
- `show_not_tell` (float): Score for sensory demonstration vs. filtering/telling words (0.0 to 10.0).
- `sensory_depth` (float): Score for visceral environmental and sensory descriptions (0.0 to 10.0).
- `pacing` (float): Score for cadence and structural sentence variation (0.0 to 10.0).
- `dialogue_realism` (float): Score for naturalistic speech markers and contractions (0.0 to 10.0).
- `thematic_resonance` (float): Score for motif alignment and thematic depth (0.0 to 10.0).
- `prose_rhythm` (float): Score for lyrical balance of short and long cadences (0.0 to 10.0).
- `overall_score` (float): Weighted composite score across all 8 criteria (0.0 to 10.0).
- `passes_used` (int): Number of revision iterations performed (1 to 3).

#### Validation Rules & Constraints
- All individual criteria scores and the `overall_score` must be between 0.0 and 10.0.
- `passes_used` must be between 1 and 3.

---

### 3. Manuscript Draft (`ManuscriptDraft`)
Represents the story text undergoing iterative generation, evaluation, revision, and anti-slop filtering.

#### Fields
- `draft_id` (UUID): Unique identifier for the draft version.
- `chapter_number` (int): The sequential chapter number being drafted.
- `initial_prose` (str): The raw text generated by the initial LLM writer pass.
- `revised_prose` (str): The updated text resulting from the Quality Engine revision loop.
- `final_prose` (str): The polished text after passing through the Anti-Slop filter layer.
- `quality_score` (float): The final overall quality score achieved.
- `is_approved` (bool): Flag indicating whether the author has accepted the draft.

#### State Transitions
`INITIAL_DRAFT_GENERATED` → `EVALUATED` → `REVISED` (up to 3 passes) → `ANTI_SLOP_FILTERED` → `STREAMED_TO_CLIENT` → `APPROVED_BY_AUTHOR`.
